Module 5: Supporting physical development in the early years

Physical development is central to children’s all-round development. This is why it is one of the three prime areas of learning in the early years foundation stage (EYFS). Physical activity helps children to master control of their body and build healthy habits. As an early years practitioner, you will help children develop the physical, fine and gross motor skills they need to thrive.

This module covers:

  • the typical stages of physical development in the early years
  • potential factors that can affect physical development
  • how physical skills impact on learning, development and behaviour
  • the importance of the curriculum, environment and relationships for promoting physical development
