Understanding child development and the EYFS

This module has 2 sections. You must complete the sections and the topics in those sections in the order shown. You must complete a section before you can start the next one.

As an early years practitioner, understanding how children develop will enable you to meet the needs of all the children in your care. This module will help you to reflect on why knowledge of child development is important and help you to effectively implement the EYFS. It also considers the impact of the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic on children.
