Brain development in the early years

This course has 15 topics. You must complete the topics in the order shown. You must complete a topic before you can start the next one.

How does it work?

Check your learning

Throughout the course there are activities to check you understand what you have learned. You will be able to see if you get these questions right or wrong. The scores for these activities are not recorded and they do not count towards the certificate of achievement.

Saving your progress

Your progress is automatically saved as you move through the module. You can sign out or close the browser at any time without losing your progress. When you next login, you can restart from where you left off.

End of module quiz

Once you have completed all of the topics, there is an end of module quiz. This end of module quiz is not aimed to provide a pass or fail. It is here for you to revisit what you have learned and the impact on your practice.

If you want to receive a certificate of achievement for this module, you need to receive an overall score of at least 66%.

If you do not score 66%, you will be able to see which questions you got wrong. You can repeat the test to improve your score as many times as you want to.
