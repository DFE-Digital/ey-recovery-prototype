Case study: Sarah plays peeakaboo

Childminder Chris is playing with 6 month old Sarah. Chris is covering a teddy with a blanket. When the Teddy is covered, Sarah immediately pulls off the blanket to find it and giggles.



What did Piaget call the concept of understanding an object which you cannot see still exists?

That's right.

Object permanence is the ability to mentally picture an item even when it was no longer there.
